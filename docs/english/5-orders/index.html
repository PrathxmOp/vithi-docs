    Chapter 5: Processing Orders (Step-by-Step)#

    When a customer places an order:

    1. New Order Alert: You will receive a loud sound notification on your Seller App.
    2. Accepting: Open the order and click Accept. This notifies the customer and automatically requests a nearby Vithi delivery rider.
    3. Preparation: Prepare and pack the items securely.
      • For Food: Ensure packaging keeps the food hot and spill-proof.
      • For Grocery/Fashion: Securely pack items in carry bags/boxes.
    4. Ready for Pickup: Once packed, click Mark as Prepared / Ready.
    5. Handover: When the Vithi delivery rider arrives, verify the order ID on their screen and hand over the package.

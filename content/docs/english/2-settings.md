# Chapter 2: Configuring Store Settings

Once logged in, navigate to **Settings > Store Profile** to complete your setup:

*   **Logo & Banner:** Upload a clear, high-resolution logo (1:1 aspect ratio) and a professional store banner (16:9 aspect ratio). Clean images attract 70% more customers!
*   **Location & Delivery Zones:** Pin your store's exact location on the map and choose the delivery zones you operate in.
*   **Delivery Time:** Set your average preparation/estimated delivery time (e.g., 20-30 mins for food, 1 hour for groceries).
*   **Opening Hours:** Set your shop's open and close times for each day of the week.

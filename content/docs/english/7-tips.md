# Chapter 7: Best Practices for Boosting Sales

To increase orders and build a loyal customer base:

*   **Keep Prices Competitive:** Offer small discounts or coupons to attract first-time buyers.
*   **Accurate Stock:** Keep your product stock updated to avoid order cancellations. High cancellation rates reduce your store's visibility in the app.
*   **Timely Preparation:** Hand over packages to riders quickly. Stores with faster preparation times get boosted in Vithi's search algorithms!
*   **Stunning Images:** Always use clear pictures showing the actual product.

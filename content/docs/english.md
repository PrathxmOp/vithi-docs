## 1. Store Registration & Setup
To start selling on Vithi:
1. Download the **Vithi Seller App** or visit the **Vithi Seller Portal** (`seller.vithi.in`).
2. Click on **Register Store** or **Become a Partner**.
3. Fill in your details:
   - Owner Name & Contact Number.
   - Store Name & Store Type (e.g. Restaurant, Grocery, Clothing).
   - Email Address.
4. Set a strong password and submit your application.
5. Once your application is reviewed and approved by the Vithi Admin, you will receive an SMS and email notification confirming that your account is active.

## 2. Configuring Store Settings
Once logged in, navigate to **Settings > Store Profile** to complete your setup:
*   **Logo & Banner:** Upload a clear, high-resolution logo (1:1 aspect ratio) and a professional store banner (16:9 aspect ratio). Clean images attract 70% more customers!
*   **Location & Delivery Zones:** Pin your store's exact location on the map and choose the delivery zones you operate in.
*   **Delivery Time:** Set your average preparation/estimated delivery time (e.g., 20-30 mins for food, 1 hour for groceries).
*   **Opening Hours:** Set your shop's open and close times for each day of the week.

## 3. How to Add Products & Categories
Correctly categorizing and naming your products makes it easy for customers to find them.

### Step-by-Step Guide to Adding a Product:
1. Navigate to the **Products** tab in your dashboard and click **Add New Product**.
2. **Product Title:** Use clear, descriptive names (e.g., *Amul Gold Milk 500ml* or *Cheese Grilled Sandwich*).
3. **Short Description:** Mention ingredients, weight, size, or material (e.g., *Crispy grilled sandwich loaded with fresh veggies and mozzarella cheese*).
4. **Choose Parent Category:**
   - **Food & Meals:** For cooked, ready-to-eat foods (Thalis, Fast Food, Cakes, Sweets, etc.).
   - **Grocery & Essentials:** For raw ingredients, milk packets, masalas, pantry staples, toiletries.
   - **Fashion & Beauty:** For clothes, makeup, skincare, jewelry.
   - **Electronics & Gadgets:** For smartwatches, chargers, headphones, phone cases.
   - **Pharma & Wellness:** For medicines, diapers, health supplements.
5. **Select Subcategory:** Choose the specific subcategory (e.g., *Snacks & Fast Food* under Food & Meals).
6. **Price & Stock:** Enter the regular price, discount price (optional), and the available inventory.
7. **Product Image:** Upload a high-quality product photo. Avoid blurry photos or white placeholders.

## 4. Managing Product Variants (Attributes) & Add-ons

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Pre-requisite:** Before you can add variants (sizes/colors) or add-ons to a product, you must first define them in their respective sections in your dashboard menu.

### Step 1: Create Attributes & Add-ons First
*   **For Variants (e.g. Size, Weight, Color):**
    1. Go to the dashboard menu and select **Attributes** (or **Product Settings > Attributes**).
    2. Add new attributes like *Size*, *Weight*, or *Color*.
*   **For Add-ons (e.g. Extra Cheese, Extra Pav, Cold Drink combo):**
    1. Go to the dashboard menu and select **Add-ons**.
    2. Click **Add New Add-on**, enter its name (e.g., *Extra Cheese*), set its price, and click Save.

### Step 2: Link them to your Product
1. When adding or editing a product, scroll down to the **Attributes & Add-ons** section.
2. Select the checkbox for the attributes you want to use (e.g., *Size*).
3. Choose the values (e.g., *Small, Medium, Large*).
4. Set the specific price and stock for each combination under **Variant Combinations**.
5. Select the **Add-ons** you want to offer with this product from the dropdown.
6. Click **Save**.

## 5. Processing Orders (Step-by-Step)
When a customer places an order:
1. **New Order Alert:** You will receive a loud sound notification on your Seller App.
2. **Accepting:** Open the order and click **Accept**. This notifies the customer and automatically requests a nearby Vithi delivery rider.
3. **Preparation:** Prepare and pack the items securely.
   - *For Food:* Ensure packaging keeps the food hot and spill-proof.
   - *For Grocery/Fashion:* Securely pack items in carry bags/boxes.
4. **Ready for Pickup:** Once packed, click **Mark as Prepared / Ready**.
5. **Handover:** When the Vithi delivery rider arrives, verify the order ID on their screen and hand over the package.

## 6. Earnings & Wallet Payouts
*   **Tracking Earnings:** View daily, weekly, and monthly sales summaries under the **Earnings/Wallet** tab.
*   **Commission:** Vithi automatically deducts the agreed platform commission before depositing funds.
*   **Requesting Payouts:** You can request a payout of your wallet balance to your registered bank account directly from the app. Payout requests are processed within 24-48 business hours.

---

## 7. Best Practices for Boosting Sales
To increase orders and build a loyal customer base:
*   **Keep Prices Competitive:** Offer small discounts or coupons to attract first-time buyers.
*   **Accurate Stock:** Keep your product stock updated to avoid order cancellations. High cancellation rates reduce your store's visibility in the app.
*   **Timely Preparation:** Hand over packages to riders quickly. Stores with faster preparation times get boosted in Vithi's search algorithms!
*   **Stunning Images:** Always use clear pictures showing the actual product.
